About Batticaloa Public Library

📖 Library Overview

Established in 1945, Batticaloa Public Library is the cultural heart of the Eastern Province. We serve 50,000+ registered members with over 120,000 physical items and growing digital resources.

✨ Vision & Mission

Vision: Empowering communities through inclusive access to information.
Mission: To foster literacy, lifelong learning, and preserve local heritage.

🏛️ History

Rebuilt after the 1980s destruction, the library today symbolizes resilience. It was renovated in 2015 with modern facilities and digital infrastructure.

📜 Rules & Regulations

Loans: 3 weeks for Lending, 1 renewal.
Fines: LKR 20/day overdue.
Membership: Free for students, LKR 500 annual fee for adults.

📍 Branches

Kallady Branch Library & Community Centre • Kattankudy Digital Reading Room • Pulliyanthivu Mobile Library Unit.

Our Collections

📚 Lending Collection

Fiction, non-fiction, bestsellers, and Tamil/Sinhala/English literature available for home borrowing.

📖 Reference Collection

Encyclopedias, dictionaries, law reports, and rare reference books (in-library use).

📰 Periodicals

Daily newspapers (Daily News, Thinakaran, Virakesari), magazines, and scholarly journals.

🌟 Special Collections

Local Authors – Works of Batticaloa writers.
Sri Lankan Collection – Historical texts
Rare Books – Pre-independence documents.
