Guide To Montessori

Montessori is a child-centered learning method that encourages independence and hands-on learning. It helps children learn at their own pace in a supportive environment.

Preschool is ideal for children aged 2.5 to 5 years. This stage focuses on early development, social skills, and basic learning.

Children learn basic skills like letters, numbers, colors, and communication. They also develop social, emotional, and motor skills through activities.

Preschool builds confidence, independence, and early learning habits. It prepares children for primary school in a fun and structured way.
