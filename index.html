Guide To Montessori

What is Montessori education?

Child-centered learning that encourages independence and hands-on learning at their own pace.

What age group is suitable for preschool?

Ideal for children aged 2.5 to 5 years – focusing on early development and social skills.

What do children learn?

Letters, numbers, colors, communication, motor and emotional skills through play.

Is safety important?

Absolutely – secure, clean, supervised environment with CCTV and trained staff.
